# ITHAR Brand Guidelines

> ITHAR (إيثار) — Clothing for the Risers.
> These guidelines are non-negotiable. Every touchpoint must honor them.

---

## 1. Brand Essence

**Name:** ITHAR (إيثار)
**Meaning:** Selflessness — to prefer others over oneself. The highest form of devotion.
**Tagline:** Clothing for the Risers.
**Inspiration:** The values, legacy, and sacrifices of the Ahlulbayt (ع).

### Brand Values (in order of prominence)
1. Loyalty (وفاء)
2. Justice (عدل)
3. Courage (شجاعة)
4. Patience (صبر)
5. Sacrifice (تضحية)
6. Hope (أمل)
7. Devotion (إخلاص)

### Brand Archetype
**The Hero / The Sage** — ITHAR doesn't sell clothing. It carries a message. Every piece is a statement of allegiance, a wearable principle. The customer isn't a consumer; they are a Riser — someone who embodies the values of the brand.

## 2. Logo System

### Primary Logo
- **Elements:** Arabic calligraphic إيثار + mosque/minaret motif + crescent moon + "ITHAR" wordmark + "إيثار" Arabic + "CLOTHING FOR THE RISERS" tagline
- **Color:** Antique gold (#B89A67) on black. Never reversed without approval.
- **Minimum size:** 120px wide (digital), 25mm (print)
- **Clear space:** Equal to the height of the "I" in ITHAR on all sides

### Secondary Mark (313)
- **Elements:** "313" numeral inside 8-pointed Islamic star
- **Usage:** Embroidery patches, secondary branding, packaging stamps
- **Never use:** As the primary identifier without the full logo nearby

### Tertiary Mark (Hamsa)
- **Elements:** Hand/Hamsa silhouette with إيثار inside
- **Usage:** Small-scale applications, social media avatars, hang tags

### Logo Don'ts
- ❌ Never stretch or distort any mark
- ❌ Never use on cluttered backgrounds
- ❌ Never change the gold to any other color without explicit approval
- ❌ Never add drop shadows to the logo
- ❌ Never place on backgrounds lighter than #3A3A3A without a dark panel underneath
- ❌ Never use on white backgrounds (off-white #EAE2D6 minimum, with care)
- ❌ Never use decorative fonts around the logo marks
- ❌ Never recreate the Arabic calligraphy — use only approved files

## 3. Color Usage

### Dominance Rules
1. **Black (#0B0B0B)** — 75% of any composition
2. **Charcoal (#1A1A1A)** — 15% — secondary surfaces
3. **Gold (#B89A67)** — 8% — the premium signal
4. **Off-White (#EAE2D6)** — 2% — text, fine details

### Color Don'ts
- ❌ Never use bright white (#FFFFFF) on dark surfaces — always off-white
- ❌ Never use bright gold (#FFD700) — only antique gold
- ❌ Never use solid gold as a background for large areas
- ❌ Never use colors outside the palette without sign-off
- ❌ Never use gradients except: black-to-transparent overlays on images

## 4. Typography Rules

### Hierarchy
```
Level 1 (Hero):      Cormorant Garamond — very large, tight, editorial
Level 2 (Section):   Cormorant Garamond — medium, confident
Level 3 (Labels):    Inter — small, uppercase, tracked
Level 4 (Body):      Inter — regular weight, generous line-height
Level 5 (Arabic):    Noto Naskh Arabic / Amiri — for Arabic text only
```

### Typography Don'ts
- ❌ Never use bold (700+) on Cormorant for hero text — use 300-400 weight for luxury
- ❌ Never center-align body paragraphs over 2 lines
- ❌ Never use ALL CAPS on display headings
- ❌ Never mix more than 2 font families on one page section
- ❌ Never set Arabic text in Latin fonts
- ❌ Minimum body text size: 15px (never smaller for readability)

### Copywriting Voice
- **Declarative.** Not persuasive. The brand speaks with authority, not desperation.
- **Short sentences.** Maximum 15 words for hero copy.
- **Present tense.** "ITHAR is." Not "ITHAR will be."
- **No hyperbole.** No "the best," "amazing," "incredible." Let the product speak.
- **Sacred references.** Treat Ahlulbayt references with absolute reverence. Never casual.
- **Bilingual awareness.** Arabic phrases must be accurate. Always verify with a scholar.

### Copy Examples
```
✅ "Heavyweight. Purposeful. Worn by those who rise."
✅ "The Wilayah Hoodie. 450 GSM. A statement of allegiance."
✅ "From Ghadir. For eternity."

❌ "Amazing quality hoodie you'll love!"
❌ "Check out our new drop!"
❌ "Perfect gift for anyone!"
```

## 5. Photography Direction

### Style Principles
- **Dark and cinematic.** Shoot in low/dramatic light. Black is the canvas.
- **Single subject.** One garment, one model, one intention per frame.
- **Architectural framing.** Use doorways, archways, corridors — structural elements.
- **No lifestyle clichés.** No laughing, no coffee cups, no jumping.
- **Sacred stillness.** Models hold themselves with dignity and composure.
- **Detail shots are required.** Fabric texture, stitching, hardware, embroidery close-ups.

### Lighting
- **Key:** Single dramatic source — side-lit or top-lit
- **Fill:** Near-zero fill. Embrace shadows.
- **Background:** Near-black or concrete/stone surfaces
- **Avoid:** Ring lights, flat studio lighting, blown-out whites

### Model Direction
- **Posture:** Upright, grounded, intentional
- **Expression:** Calm, focused — not smiling for camera
- **Movement:** Slow, deliberate — if any
- **Ethnicity:** Diverse but always authentic to the community ITHAR serves

### Post-Processing
- **Tone:** Desaturated, moody — pull shadows to near-black
- **Contrast:** High — let blacks be black
- **Skin tone:** Warm but not orange, natural
- **No filters:** No presets that add artificial grain or color grading beyond the above

## 6. Arabic Calligraphy Usage

### The Calligraphy
The Arabic text on the Wilayah Hoodie:
```
من كنت مولاه
فهذا علي مولاه
```
*"Whoever I am his master, then Ali is his master."*
— Prophet Muhammad ﷺ at Ghadir Khumm, 10 AH

### Rules for Sacred Text
- ✅ Always render accurately — zero tolerance for errors
- ✅ Maintain the calligraphic style from approved files
- ✅ Always include "GHADIR 10 AH / ITHAR" attribution below
- ✅ On digital: use the approved SVG/PNG from brand assets
- ❌ Never resize Arabic text disproportionately
- ❌ Never use any color for the calligraphy other than gold (#B89A67 / #D4AF37)
- ❌ Never place calligraphy over busy backgrounds — always on solid dark
- ❌ Never translate without including the original Arabic

## 7. Packaging & Product Identity

### Packaging Language
- Black matte boxes, gold foil logo
- Off-white tissue paper with gold stamp
- Black branded tape
- Handwritten thank-you cards (or premium printed equivalent)

### Tags & Labels
- Woven labels: black background, gold thread
- Hang tags: thick black card, gold foil, with brief brand manifesto excerpt
- Care labels: minimal, black and white

---

## 8. Digital Brand Standards

### Website
- Navigation: always transparent-on-dark initially, blur on scroll
- Loading: fade in, never flash of unstyled content
- Cursor: default pointer — no custom cursors
- Scrollbar: hide custom, use native (thin) on webkit

### Social Media
- Grid: dark, editorial, cohesive — minimum 60% product shots
- Caption style: sparse, meaningful — 1-3 lines max
- Hashtags: below the fold, not in the caption body
- Story format: always full-bleed, gold text, no stickers

### Email
- Background: #0B0B0B
- Text: #EAE2D6
- Accent: #B89A67
- Font: Inter (web-safe fallback: Georgia for serif elements)
- Width: 600px max
- No background images in email clients — use solid colors
